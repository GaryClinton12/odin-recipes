Moscow Mule

A refreshing drink with ginger and lime tones

This is a light, refreshing drink best served on a warm summers day, adjust the vodka content to suit your mood

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 1/2 fresh lime
  • Fluid Ounces vodka
  • 4 fluid ounces ginger beer, or to taste
  • 2 Lime slices

Instructions

  1. Fill a tall glass with ice. Squeeze 1/2 lime over ice. Pour vodka over ice and top with ginger beer. Garnish with lime slices.
