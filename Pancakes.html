Pancakes

Good old fashioned pancakes with maple syrup and fruit

These pancakes are a healthy way to start the day, loaded with sugar, salt and fat, they're sure to give your heart the wake up call it needs to get you going each morning

Disclaimer: Eating these pancakes more than 3 times in a week has been known to cause "Bum ticker"

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 Tablespoons butter, melted

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
